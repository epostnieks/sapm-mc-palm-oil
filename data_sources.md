# Data Sources — Palm Oil Monte Carlo Simulation

Channel parameters in `mc_simulation.py` trace to the sources listed here.
The paper (Palm Oil: Substitution Impossibility) is available on SSRN and contains the full
reference list and §4 calibration narrative.

---

## Channel Sources

### `C1_carbon_climate`

Peatland drainage CO₂, land-use change. Indonesia/Malaysia fires. Sources: Carlson et al., WRI.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C2_biodiversity_habitat`

Orangutan/tiger/elephant habitat destruction. Sources: IUCN, WWF, Gaveau et al.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C3_health_haze`

Transboundary haze, 100K+ deaths in 2015. Sources: Koplitz et al. 2016, ASEAN.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C4_labor_exploitation`

Forced/child labor on plantations. Sources: Amnesty International, DOL.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C5_governance_certification_failure`

RSPO inadequacy, greenwashing. Sources: EIA, Greenpeace.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

---

## Industry Revenue (Π)

The private payoff Π = $68.0B/yr is global annual industry revenue.
Source: paper §2 and §4. See paper §DA-1 (Decision Audit Field 7) for full
revenue source documentation.
